Executive Summary
─────────────────

## Assessment Overview
CosmoLab AI conducted a structured workflow assessment covering eight of nine standard cross-team handoffs at this cosmetic contract manufacturing organization. The session captured current-state tools, pain points, and failure modes as reported by a single organizational representative.

This assessment is based on information provided in a single session. Findings should be validated with all relevant team leads before implementation.

## Key Finding
The majority of cross-team handoffs are informal, email-dependent, and lack documented owners or SLAs, creating systemic rework, production delays, and recurring compliance risk across the organization.

## Top 3 Handoff Gaps
1. Sales → R&D (Score: 1/5): No standard brief template results in 3–5 day delays before R&D can begin work, directly delaying project starts for every new client.

2. Quality → All Teams / CAPA (Score: 1/5): Manual email-based CAPA tracking with no system enforcement means corrective actions are routinely delayed and QA staff spend significant time on follow-up rather than quality work.

3. R&D → Manufacturing (Score: 2/5): A ~30% first-pass rework rate due to missing specs adds a week or more of delay to every affected batch, representing the single largest measurable productivity loss identified.

## Top 3 Recommended Actions
1. Deploy a mandatory Sales brief intake form immediately — zero cost, implementable within one week, eliminates the primary cause of R&D start delays.

2. Activate CAPA tracking in the existing QMS — system is already owned; activation eliminates manual follow-up burden on QA and reduces compliance audit exposure.

3. Introduce an R&D → Manufacturing scale-up checklist — directly targets the 30% rework rate; expected to recover 4–6 hours per week in manufacturing and R&D time combined.

## Next Steps
• Schedule a 60-minute validation session with R&D, Quality, Demand Planning, and Manufacturing team leads to confirm findings and assign document owners.
• Prioritize Month 1 Stabilize actions; brief template and QMS CAPA activation can begin within 5 business days.
• Assign a single internal project owner to track 90-day roadmap progress and report monthly to leadership.
