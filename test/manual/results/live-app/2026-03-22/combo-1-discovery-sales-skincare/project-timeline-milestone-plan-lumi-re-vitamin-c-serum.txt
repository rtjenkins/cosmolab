Project Timeline & Milestone Plan – Lumière Vitamin C Serum
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

## Phase 1 – Formulation Development (Weeks 1–8)
• Week 1–2: Formula brief sign-off and clean beauty ingredient screening
• Week 4–6: Initial prototype development
• Week 7–8: Internal evaluation and client prototype submission

## Phase 2 – Testing & Compliance (Weeks 9–18)
• Week 9–10: Stability and compatibility testing initiation
• Week 12–14: Claims substantiation review (brightening, antioxidant)
• Week 15–18: US and Canada regulatory/labeling compliance review

## Phase 3 – Production & Launch (Months 6–8)
• Month 6–7: Formula lock, artwork approval, and bulk production for 3,000-unit run
• Month 8: Finished goods delivery, Q4 US launch target met
• Month 14: Canada launch (6 months post-US), 10,000-unit scale production

## Key Risks & Mitigations
• Risk: Vitamin C stability issues — Mitigation: evaluate multiple vitamin C forms early in development
• Risk: Timeline compression — Mitigation: parallel-path stability and regulatory review where possible
